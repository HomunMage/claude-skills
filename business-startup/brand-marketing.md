## Core Philosophy

**Profit-first mindset** — every action must prioritize revenue generation:

1. **Fundraise / get paid BEFORE building** — pre-sales, subscriptions, crowdfunding
2. **Paying customers = best market validation** — if people pay, the market is real
3. **High-frequency updates to subscribers** — retain paying users, build trust
4. **Keep iterating the funnel & flywheel** — never stop optimizing conversion

### Money Triangle (absolute order)
When stuck at any level, look at ALL levels below:
1. Mindset & Resilience
2. Skills & Knowledge
3. Business Model
4. Revenue Streams

## Market Analysis Frameworks

### SEO
- E-E-A-T principle (Google)
- Keyword traffic = search volume × CTR (ranking)
- SEA + SEO = SEM
  - Paid ads (broad, short-term) vs Content (precise TA, long-term)
- Search intent → TA-driven, problem-solving oriented

### STP (Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning)
- Define market segments
- Select target segments
- Position product/brand

### UVP (Unique Value Proposition)
- What makes your offering uniquely valuable
- "乾我屁事" principle — why should the audience care?

### TA (Target Audience)
- Build TA table (from《普通人的財富自由之道》)
- 1,000 True Fans theory (Kevin Kelly)
- Understand platform algorithms and trends per era

## Marketing Execution

### Content Structure
- Hook → AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
- PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solution)
- CTA strategy

### 3H Funnel
- **Hero**: Professional, deep insight content
- **Hub**: Approachable, practical content
- **Hygiene**: Simple, quick-answer content

### Flywheel Effect
- Content → Audience → Trust → Revenue → More Content
- Compound growth over time (like No Man's Sky's 40 free updates)

### Cross-platform Strategy (一魚多吃)
- Same material, different platform adaptations
- Long video → Short video → Article → Podcast
- At least 3 platforms, 5+ pieces each (羞愧法則: ship imperfect work)

## Business Strategy

### Risk Management
- Platform algorithm changes → diversify platforms
- Time pressure → identify quick-output formats
- Tool limitations → have alternatives ready

### Exploration & Reverse Engineering
- Output-driven exploration (not knowledge hoarding)
- Analyze competitors: content structure, funnel design, community management
- Extract success patterns and adapt

### Management Cycles
- **OKR**: Each objective with 2-4 key results
- **AAR**: Quarterly review (see AAR.md)
- **PDCA**: Plan-Do-Check-Act continuous improvement
